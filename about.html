About TechPlus Support

Building resilient IT infrastructure for modern corporations.

Your Enterprise IT Partner

Founded in 2014 in Mumbai, TechPlus Support began with a mission to demystify business technology. Today, we are one of India's leading boutique Managed IT Service Providers, powering operations for small-to-midsize corporations and financial firms.

We believe that IT shouldn't just be reactive. Our team works proactively to maintain, upgrade, and safeguard your systems so your workforce can focus on high-value business objectives without worrying about tech outages.

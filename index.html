Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our Managed IT services and customer support policies.

What are Managed IT Services?

Managed IT Services allow a business to outsource their IT operations to a specialized provider like TechPlus Support. We proactively monitor, manage, and secure your systems, hardware, networks, and cloud instances, rather than just fixing items when they break.

How fast does your helpdesk respond to issues?

Our standard SLA provides a response within 15 minutes for critical server/network issues, and under 1 hour for standard workstation helpdesk tickets. We offer remote and telephone assistance instantly, and onsite dispatch if required.

Do you offer support on weekends and holidays?

Yes. TechPlus Support offers 24/7/365 active monitoring and technical support for businesses subscribed to our Enterprise/Managed IT plans. Our network operations center is always staffed to keep your business running.

Are there any long-term contract lock-ins?

We offer flexible terms, including month-to-month service agreements, as well as discounted annual commitments. We believe in earning your business every single month through high-quality support and engineering.
